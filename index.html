How we came to be

In life, the goal is to always keep ourselves on track irrespective of how chaotic things get. Very many times, and understandably so, we fall short of this goal.

In an attempt to get the youth back in line, Track was born. We are a group of dynamic youth that believe illustrative fashion can impact the lives of many other youths in Uganda.

We hope to use fashion to send a message and become a household name.

It Get's Crazy Sometimes

Life can get overwhelming for everyone regardless of who you are or where you are from. This design is meant to address mental health issues by letting people know that they are not alone and ought not to give up. We want to make them believe that it gets crazy for everyone but the best thing to do is to wear a good t-shirt and soldier on.

Fearless

If there is anything that women, young or old, have in common, it's being Fearless. With all the trials and tribulations that life has thrown at them, they continue to break barries and rewrite history. For centuries on end, women have fought the good fight to make the world a better place for the girl child tomorrow. They are not afraid anymore to take a stand and that's something that ought to be recognized. This one is for you, Thank you for being Fearless.
